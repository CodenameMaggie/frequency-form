## Revenue Strategy Breakdown

### Strategy 1: Virtual Style Consultations ($400 target)
**Price:** $50-100 per consultation
**Goal:** 5-8 consultations

**What You Offer:**
- 60-minute video consultation
- Body type analysis & measurements
- Custom garment recommendations
- Natural fiber education
- Style guide PDF deliverable

**Day 1-3 Actions:**
1. Create booking page (use Calendly or built-in scheduling)
2. Post to social media: "Limited spots: Personal Style Consultation"
3. Target: Fashion bloggers, sustainable fashion enthusiasts
4. Offer early bird special: $50 (normally $100)

**Where to Promote:**
- Instagram Stories with booking link
- Pinterest Pins with "Book Now" links
- LinkedIn post targeting professional women
- Facebook groups: Sustainable Fashion, Natural Living

### Strategy 2: Pre-Orders for Signature Pieces ($300 target)
**Price:** $150-200 per garment
**Goal:** 2-3 pre-orders

**What You Offer:**
- Custom-fitted linen dresses
- Made-to-order with 3-4 week delivery
- 50% deposit required ($75-100 per order)
- Partner with existing seamstress from database

**Day 1-5 Actions:**
1. Select 3 signature designs (simple, elegant, sellable)
2. Take/create product photos
3. Write compelling product descriptions
4. Set up pre-order page with deposit payment
5. Create urgency: "Limited Spring Collection - 10 pieces only"

**Where to Promote:**
- Annie Pinterest bot: Post daily to relevant boards
- Instagram: Lifestyle + fashion photos
- Email your network: "Exclusive first access"
- Tag sustainable fashion influencers
