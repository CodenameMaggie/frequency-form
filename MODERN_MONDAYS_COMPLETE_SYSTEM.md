### Partner Product Requirements

When partners join, they MUST provide for each product:

#### 1. Professional Photography (Minimum 5 images per product)
- Front view (on model or flat lay)
- Back view
- Detail shots (fabric texture, stitching, buttons)
- Lifestyle shot (styled/worn)
- Size/fit reference (if applicable)

**Image Specs:**
- Minimum resolution: 2000x2000px
- Format: JPG or PNG
- White or neutral background preferred
- Consistent lighting across all images

#### 2. Exact Fabric Composition (REQUIRED)
**Format:** Must list every material with percentage

**Examples:**
- ✅ "100% Italian Linen"
- ✅ "95% Organic Cotton, 5% Elastane"
- ✅ "70% Merino Wool, 30% Cashmere"
- ❌ "Cotton blend" (too vague)
- ❌ "Natural fibers" (not specific)

**Auto-Reject if contains:**
- Polyester
- Nylon
- Acrylic
- Rayon
- Viscose/Modal
- Any synthetic material

#### 3. Frequency Calculation
**Option A: Auto-Calculated (Our System)**
- We use fabric composition to calculate frequency
- Healing Tier (5,000 Hz): 100% Linen/Wool/Cashmere/Hemp/Silk
- Foundation Tier (100 Hz): 100% Organic Cotton
- Blends: Weighted average based on percentages

**Option B: Seller-Provided (If Certified)**
- Lab-tested frequency from Dr. Heidi Yellen or certified testing facility
- Upload certificate/documentation
- We verify and use their number
- Display "Lab-Verified Frequency" badge

**Special Validation:**
- **Linen/Wool Mix Check**: Auto-reject if product contains both linen AND wool
  - Reason: Frequencies cancel to zero (ancient textile principle)
  - Exception: Different garments worn on different days (OK)

#### 4. Complete Sizing Information
- Full size range (XS-3XL or specific measurements)
- Size chart with exact measurements (chest, waist, hip, length, etc.)
- Fit notes (runs small, true to size, oversized, etc.)
- Model measurements + size worn in photos

#### 5. Color Variations
- All available colorways
- Separate photos for each color
- Color names (not just "blue" - specify "Navy" or "Cerulean")
- Hex codes if possible (for accurate rendering)

#### 6. Stock Levels & Pricing
- Real-time inventory for each size/color combo
- Wholesale cost (what we pay)
- Suggested retail price
- Minimum order quantity (if applicable)

#### 7. Product Description
- Detailed description (150-300 words)
- Key features (breathable, lightweight, wrinkle-resistant, etc.)
- Care instructions
- Origin story (where made, artisan details)
- Why this product elevates energy (connect to frequency)

## Part 4: Dan's Lead Generation Automation

### What Dan Does

**1. Natural Fiber Brand Discovery**
- Scrapes Instagram, Pinterest, Etsy for keywords:
  - #linen #organicfabric #naturalfibers #cashmere #sustainablefashion
  - #madeinItaly #Frenchfashion #europeanstyle
- Filters by:
  - Follower count (1K-50K sweet spot)
  - Engagement rate (>2%)
  - Product quality (professional photos)
  - No synthetics visible

**2. Qualifying Scores**
Dan scores each brand 0-100 based on:
- **Aesthetic alignment** (30 pts): Does it match F&F style? (Classy, European, timeless)
- **Natural fiber commitment** (30 pts): All products natural? Any synthetics visible?
- **Product range** (20 pts): 10+ SKUs? Varied categories?
- **Production capacity** (10 pts): Can they fulfill orders? Look established?
- **Social proof** (10 pts): Reviews, testimonials, press features?

**Threshold:**
- 85+ = Immediate podcast invitation
- 70-84 = Monitor for 2 weeks, then invite
- <70 = Skip

**3. Automated Outreach**
For brands scoring 85+, Dan sends personalized email:

```
Subject: [Brand Name] Featured on Modern Mondays Podcast?

Hi [Founder Name],

I'm Dan, Marketing Director at Frequency & Form, and I discovered [Brand Name] through your stunning [specific product post] on Instagram.

Your [fabric] [product type] is exactly the kind of classy, European-inspired natural fiber fashion we celebrate on Modern Mondays - our podcast reaching 10K+ conscious shoppers every week.

Would you be interested in being featured as a guest? We'd showcase:
- Your brand story & design philosophy
- 3-5 signature pieces (with direct links for listeners)
- Your commitment to natural fibers & timeless style

Plus, our most engaged guests often join our curated marketplace, driving ongoing sales through frequencyandform.com.

Book your spot: [Calendly link]

Looking forward to featuring [Brand Name]!

Dan Thompson
Marketing Director, Frequency & Form
```

**4. Follow-Up Sequence**
- Day 0: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Reminder (if no response)
- Day 7: Final follow-up with social proof ("Recent guests saw 20% traffic boost")
- Day 10: Archive (move to low-priority list)

**5. Pipeline Management**
Dan tracks in GMP:
- Brands contacted
- Response rate
- Podcast bookings scheduled
- Episodes recorded
- Partner applications submitted
- Approved partners
