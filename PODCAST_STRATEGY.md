**Core Concept:** A holistic lifestyle approach that recognizes what surrounds us (fabrics, materials, systems, environments) shapes our energy, health, and sovereignty.

### Pillars of Sovereign Living

1. **Natural Fiber Clothing** (Frequency & Form)

   - No synthetics ever
   - Linen/wool separation principle

2. **Timber Frame Homes**
   - Natural building materials
   - Energy-efficient design
   - Lasting construction methods
   - Connection to land and materials

3. **Self-Sufficiency Systems**
   - Homesteading practices
   - Regenerative agriculture
   - Food independence
   - Water and energy systems

4. **Lasting & Healthy Systems**
   - Regenerative design principles
   - Ancestral wisdom meets modern science
   - Quality over quantity

5. **Sovereign Entrepreneurship**
   - Building businesses aligned with natural principles

   - Community-focused economics
   - Sustainable business models

## Guest Experience & Process

### Step 1: Guest Books via Calendly
- Automated confirmation email
- Pre-interview questionnaire sent
- Calendar invite with Zoom link

### Step 2: Pre-Interview Prep
- 10-15 minute planning call
- Discuss topics, flow, key stories
- Technical setup verification
- Send talking points outline

### Step 3: Recording Session
- 45-60 minute conversation (Zoom)
- Casual, authentic dialogue
- No script, just conversation prompts
- Record audio + video (1080p minimum)

### Step 4: Post-Production
- Professional editing (remove filler, improve flow)
- Add intro/outro music
- Create clips and graphics
- Write show notes and transcript

### Step 5: Publishing & Promotion
- Schedule episode release
- Send guest preview link
- Provide shareable assets to guest
- Cross-promote on all platforms
- Email to subscriber list

### Step 6: Guest Appreciation
- Thank you email with all links
- Send traffic/download stats after 30 days
- Maintain relationship for future episodes
- Introduce to other guests when relevant
