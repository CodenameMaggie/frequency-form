
- Follower count: 1K - 100K (sweet spot)
- Engagement rate: 2%+
- Bio mentions: natural fibers, organic, sustainable, handmade
- Products clearly show fabric type
- Professional product photography

## FINDING CONTACT INFO

### Priority Order:

1. **Direct email** (best)
   - partnerships@brandname.com
   - hello@brandname.com
   - info@brandname.com
   - founder name from About page

2. **Contact form** (good)
   - Use if no email available
   - Keep message short, ask for partnership contact

3. **Instagram DM** (okay)
   - Professional message
   - Ask for email to discuss partnership

4. **LinkedIn** (last resort)
   - Find founder/owner
   - Send connection request with note

## PERSONALIZATION RESEARCH (5 mins per brand)

Before emailing, note:

1. **Specific product** they make
   - "I loved your linen bedding collection"
   - "Your organic cotton basics line is beautiful"

2. **Brand story element**
   - From their About page
   - Founder's name
   - Origin story
   - Mission statement

3. **Recent achievement**
   - Instagram post about a milestone
   - New product launch
   - Feature in a publication

**Use this in email:**
```
"I came across [Brand Name] and was immediately drawn to your
[specific product]. The [quality/mission/story element] really
resonates with what we're building."
```

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Personalize every email** - No copy/paste
2. **Follow up 2-3 times** - Most people need reminders
3. **Be genuine** - You're building partnerships, not making sales
4. **Move fast** - Respond to replies within 2 hours
5. **Quality > quantity** - Better to send 5 great emails than 20 mediocre ones
6. **Track everything** - You'll forget who you contacted
7. **Learn and iterate** - Track open rates, adjust subject lines
